Remove the Middle Man

TextEx removes the bookstore middle man by helping students buy and sell textbooks directly to and from each other. Now the book store cannot take a cut from your resale! 100% of the sale goes back to you!

The current market information for each book is presented in an intuitive, user-friendly format. All book offers and requests are presented in a simple tabular format with sorting capabilities to help you find that ideal copy with the click of a few buttons.

Name Your Own Price

Sellers can name their own price to match the current demand. Now it is the students who dictate the market price of each textbook, not the publisher.
